Who We Are

Skye African Supermarket is a trusted African grocery store located at 16 The Square, Wolverton, Milton Keynes, MK12 5DG. We specialise in providing high-quality African food products and everyday groceries, serving individuals, families, and local businesses in the Milton Keynes area. Our supermarket offers a wide selection of authentic African ingredients, including dry foods, spices, frozen items, and essential household products. We are proud to support diverse cultures by making traditional African foods easily accessible to our community. At Skye African Supermarket, customer satisfaction is at the heart of everything we do. We focus on affordability, quality, and friendly service, creating a welcoming shopping experience for everyone who visits our store.

Our Mission

To make authentic African groceries and flavours accessible across the UK — responsibly sourced, affordably priced and delivered with care.

Our Vision

To be the UK’s most-loved destination for African food, connecting communities with the tastes of home while celebrating culinary diversity.

Our Promise

Quality produce, transparent sourcing, and reliable delivery — because trust matters in every basket we deliver.
